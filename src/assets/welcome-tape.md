You can also add as many text cells as you like, to for example describe why you picked this specific song :)

---

---

Double-click any cell to edit it, and drag and drop to rearrange.

---

Text-Cells support Markdown Syntax like

## Headings

And basic formatting like **bold** or *italic*.

Also images are supported with the markdown image Syntax 
```markdown
![Image Title](image-link)
```

You can share a tape by just copying and sharing the link in your browsers URL bar, the whole tape is encoded in the URL.
